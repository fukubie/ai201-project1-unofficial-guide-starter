SECTION: SCHOLAR PROFILE AND RESEARCH FINGERPRINT
Research Keywords: Algorithms, Combinatorial Optimization, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, Bioinformatics, Computational Biology, Image Processing, Computer Vision, Transportation, Neural Network Control, Image Restoration, Image Inpainting, Field Programmable Gate Array (FPGA), Federated Machine Learning, Knowledge Distillation.
Active Research Summary: Directs the Bioinformatics and Artificial Intelligence Lab (BioAI Lab). Research focuses on solving combinatorial optimization and sequencing problems in biological data (like protein scaffold gaps), engineering deep learning methods for computer vision tasks (such as edge-guided mural image restoration), and developing neural network controllers embedded on hardware components like FPGAs. Also tracks computational biology applications utilizing federated learning networks.
