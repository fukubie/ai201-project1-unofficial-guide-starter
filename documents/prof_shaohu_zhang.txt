SECTION: SCHOLAR PROFILE AND RESEARCH FINGERPRINT
Research Keywords: Voice Assistant Security, Anonymity, Anonymization Metrics, Speaker Verification, Biometrics, Privacy Preserving Transformation, Privacy Protection, Feature Fusion, Cross-Linguistic Speech Emotion Recognition, Mobile Computing, Trustworthy AI.
Active Research Summary: Leads the X+ Intelligence Lab. Research centers heavily on security, privacy, and mobile computing. Specializes in designing robust frameworks for voice anonymity, preventing the misactivation of commercial voice assistants via speaker orientation awareness, and engineering multi-feature fusion frameworks using cross-attention Transformers for speech emotion recognition. Additionally builds instance-level privacy preservation systems for vehicular camera videos to protect pedestrian and vehicle data from re-identification.
